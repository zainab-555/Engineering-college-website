Maa Pateshwari Devi, Rajkiya Engineering College, Gonda

Maa Pateshwari Devi Rajkiya Engineering College, Gonda, established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh in 2023, as an associated Institute of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. The Institute offers four year full time B.Tech programmes in Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering, each with an annual intake of 60 students. Further, Institute has an ecosystem of innovation and startup, where students work on local problems and provide such solutions that are globally accepted. The Institute provides quality education and skills to prepare human resources that are problem solver and globally competitive.
